Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

Daily Faith is a Catholic daily devotional app. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how the app works.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Daily Faith does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not track your activity.

Network Requests

The app connects to the following third-party services to provide daily content:

  • Catholic Readings API (cpbjr.github.io) - to retrieve daily Mass reading references and liturgical calendar data
  • Bible API (bible-api.com) - to retrieve the text of the daily Gospel reading

These requests are made solely to fetch content. No personal information is sent to these services.

Notifications

Daily Faith may request permission to send you a daily reminder notification. This is entirely optional and processed locally on your device. We do not use push notification services or collect device tokens.

Analytics and Tracking

Daily Faith does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device information.

Data Storage

All app data (such as your notification preference) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Daily Faith does not integrate with any advertising networks, social media platforms, or analytics providers.

Children's Privacy

Daily Faith does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
